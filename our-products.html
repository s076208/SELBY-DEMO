OUR PRODUCTS

Aluminium Extrusions

Aluminium Extrusion was first introduced to the Maldives by our company in the early 80's. Since its introduction Aluminium has replaced the more traditional Wood framed exterior doors and Windows. We distribute the highest quality of Aluminium in Maldives.

PVC Pipe and Fittings

A wide range of PVC Pipes and fittings are available for low, medium and High Pressure application. All our products are manufactured in THAILAND.

Adhesives and Sealants

We stock a wide range of high-quality adhesives and sealants including Hardware, Sanitary/Plumbing, Construction (Door/Windows/Façade), Timber and Vinyl Flooring and more.

Tools

We stock more than 100 brands of tools including Solex, Evacut, P&N, Jetech, Greatneck, Irwin, Patta and Kincrome. All our tools are high in quality and extreme heavy duty.

Floor Coverings

Introducing Duraflor - Our Cushioned Floor Covering. Easy installation, easy to clean and maintain, safe for children and the Elderly.

Electrical & Lighting

Switches and sockets, DB boxes, MCB, ELCB, LED Lighting and accessories, Conduit wiring accessories. We have everything you need for electrical repairs.

Fasteners

The widest range of (Steel and Stainless Steel) quality fasteners available under one roof. We only stock PATTA brand of fasteners - direct from manufacturer.

Racks & Storage

We offer Gondola Racks for Supermarkets, Slim Gondola Racks for Pharmacy, and Boltless Shelving Racks for warehouse, office and homes.

Acrylic Sheets

We offer a wide variety of colors and sizes. Our acrylic sheet products can be cemented, thermoformed and machined conveniently. Resistant to chemical and weather conditions.

Laminates & Furniture Fittings

Laminates provide the Interior designer with the balance of form, function, touch of colour and personal style. Furniture fittings add much needed augmentation and convenience.

Locks and Security Products

Solex locks offer the very highest standard of quality and functional reliability with high-grade materials and precise production techniques.
